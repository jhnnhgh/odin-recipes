Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup diced onion

Steps

  1. Preheat grill or stovetop griddle to medium-high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the ground beef, egg, bread crumbs, diced onion, diced bell pepper, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  3. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape into patties.
